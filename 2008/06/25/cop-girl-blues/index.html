Cop Girl Blues (Whisky no hay más) [Spanglish for: El Blues de la Chica Policía (Whisky ain't no more )] was once called "the most infamous song ever made", probabily for having just 12 lines (Two of them quotes of a Fito Paez' song) and still managing to become a 4 minutes-long ear-tearing Brit-Latin Blues. It does have three nice guitar solos (which should be enough to explain the lenght of the song): 1st and 3rd Solos played by Gonzalo Angeriz (as usual) and the 2nd Solo (imported from the land of the Surf-Jazz) played by Julio Mollá . Despite being -really!- a very amusing song to play it runs over a quite simple motif. It does have a beat-trick, though -gab's straightforward rock'n rolls are known to bend a little at some point-.
