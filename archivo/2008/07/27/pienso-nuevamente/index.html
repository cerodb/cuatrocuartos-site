Listo! Terminamos otro temita más. "Pienso Nuevamente" del único e irrepetible Goz. El tema puede escucharse / descargarse en varios lugares: Descargando el disco entero: EP2. Me Da Igual En garageband.com - Pienso Nuevamente En ilike.com - Pienso Nuevamente \- De paso Votalo Aquí! La descripción oficial del tema en Garageband.com dice así:

"Pienso Nuevamente" (Spanish for: Therefore I -who didn't thought that I would think- seem to be thinking that I just might be thinking again) is a nice Brit-Latin-Soul Song Cartesianated by the great Gonzalo Angeriz. In the current version the song presents: Great Guitars and Solos, incredible background voices, atonishing lead singer, pumping drums, parkinsonated bass, the New Black and White Brass Orchestra (with the wind arrangements), a wind chimes set, a clock, two chimps and a rabbit. Quite an achievement for a 160 seconds song.
